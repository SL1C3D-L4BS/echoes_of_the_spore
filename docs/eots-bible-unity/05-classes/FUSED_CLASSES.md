# FUSED CLASSES -- All 12 Combinations

## Fusion Overview

Two players merge into a single entity. Roles: Pilot (movement/dash), Gunner (attack/aim). In 3P, a third player acts as Support (adds 1 Passive slot, provides callouts). In 4P, a fourth acts as Relay (adds 1 Upgrade slot, manages cooldowns).

Fusion lasts until: HP depletes, players choose to separate, or the room is cleared.

## Fusion Stat Formulas

| Stat | Formula |
|---|---|
| HP | Pilot HP + Gunner HP + 2 bonus |
| Damage | Gunner base damage x 1.25 |
| Move Speed | Pilot move speed x 0.9 |
| Dash | Pilot Crest dash behavior |
| Attack | Gunner Crest attack animation |
| Slots | Sum of both Crest layouts (cap 10) |

## The 12 Fused Classes

---

### 1. Myco-Juggernaut (Sporeling Pilot + Rootwarden Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Rooted Dash -- phase-charge that roots all enemies in path for 2s
- **HP:** 5 + 8 + 2 = 15
- **Damage:** Rootwarden base x 1.25 (medium-high)
- **Move Speed:** 7.2 x 0.9 = 6.48 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Mobile bruiser. Phase through crowds, leaving them rooted, then bash with Rootwarden attacks. Aggressive engage with defensive follow-up.
- **Strength:** Best initiation in the game. Unmatched crowd lockdown.
- **Weakness:** Gunner attacks are slow; fast enemies can dodge between roots.

### 2. Trench Spore (Rootwarden Pilot + Sporeling Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Anchor Burst -- deploy lockdown area then detonate spore AoE
- **HP:** 8 + 5 + 2 = 15
- **Damage:** Sporeling base x 1.25 (medium-high)
- **Move Speed:** 5.0 x 0.9 = 4.5 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Trap-and-shred. Anchor enemies, then unleash rapid Sporeling combos on immobilized targets.
- **Strength:** Guaranteed damage on rooted targets. Exceptional boss DPS.
- **Weakness:** Slow movement. Vulnerable to ranged poke before anchor lands.

### 3. Pollen Phantom (Sporeling Pilot + Bloomcaster Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Phantom Bloom -- dash leaves homing petal mines
- **HP:** 5 + 4 + 2 = 11
- **Damage:** Bloomcaster base x 1.25 (high)
- **Move Speed:** 7.2 x 0.9 = 6.48 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Hit-and-run zoner. Dash through enemies planting homing mines, retreat and snipe.
- **Strength:** Highest burst potential. Mines + projectiles stack damage fast.
- **Weakness:** Lowest fused HP. Two solid hits and it is over.

### 4. Spore Sniper (Bloomcaster Pilot + Sporeling Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Phase Petal -- projectiles teleport through walls
- **HP:** 4 + 5 + 2 = 11
- **Damage:** Sporeling base x 1.25 (medium-high)
- **Move Speed:** 6.0 x 0.9 = 5.4 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Safe-range assassin. Fire through walls to hit enemies without exposure.
- **Strength:** Unparalleled safety. Enemies cannot retaliate through walls.
- **Weakness:** Lower damage than direct engagement fusions. Map-dependent.

### 5. Rot Runner (Sporeling Pilot + Decayer Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Entropy Dash -- phase-run leaves decay trail draining HP
- **HP:** 5 + 6 + 2 = 13
- **Damage:** Decayer base x 1.25 (low-medium)
- **Move Speed:** 7.2 x 0.9 = 6.48 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Mobile debuffer. Sprint through enemies applying stacking debuffs, weaken then finish.
- **Strength:** Best debuff application speed. Softens rooms for the team instantly.
- **Weakness:** Low direct damage. Depends on team to capitalize on debuffs.

### 6. Blight Blur (Decayer Pilot + Sporeling Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Touch Phase -- melee teleport chain between weakened enemies
- **HP:** 6 + 5 + 2 = 13
- **Damage:** Sporeling base x 1.25 (medium-high)
- **Move Speed:** 5.8 x 0.9 = 5.22 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Chain assassin. Teleport between weakened targets for rapid multi-kills.
- **Strength:** Best room-clear speed against weakened mobs. Resets on kill.
- **Weakness:** Requires setup (enemies must be weakened first). Poor against single targets.

### 7. Siege Bloom (Rootwarden Pilot + Bloomcaster Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Fortified Storm -- rooted turret mode, 360-degree petal barrage
- **HP:** 8 + 4 + 2 = 14
- **Damage:** Bloomcaster base x 1.25 (high)
- **Move Speed:** 5.0 x 0.9 = 4.5 units/s (0 in turret mode)
- **Playstyle:** Living turret. Plant, barrage, relocate. Positional dominance.
- **Strength:** Highest sustained ranged DPS. Untouchable during turret mode if positioned well.
- **Weakness:** Completely immobile in turret mode. Flanking enemies are a death sentence.

### 8. Bastion Caster (Bloomcaster Pilot + Rootwarden Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Root Volley -- projectiles root on impact
- **HP:** 4 + 8 + 2 = 14
- **Damage:** Rootwarden base x 1.25 (medium)
- **Move Speed:** 6.0 x 0.9 = 5.4 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Control mage. Root enemies at range, then pummel them with heavy attacks.
- **Strength:** Best ranged crowd control. Roots enable easy follow-up for the whole team.
- **Weakness:** Lower damage than Siege Bloom. Control without team follow-up wastes potential.

### 9. Plague Fortress (Rootwarden Pilot + Decayer Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Decay Anchor -- lockdown zone that progressively weakens trapped enemies
- **HP:** 8 + 6 + 2 = 16
- **Damage:** Decayer base x 1.25 (low-medium)
- **Move Speed:** 5.0 x 0.9 = 4.5 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Attrition fortress. Lock enemies down, weaken them over time, outlast everything.
- **Strength:** Highest fused HP. Impossible to kill quickly. Melts bosses over long fights.
- **Weakness:** Extremely slow. Timed rooms and chase encounters are nightmares.

### 10. Wilt Wall (Decayer Pilot + Rootwarden Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Entropic Shield -- absorbs damage, converts to AoE debuff burst
- **HP:** 6 + 8 + 2 = 16
- **Damage:** Rootwarden base x 1.25 (medium)
- **Move Speed:** 5.8 x 0.9 = 5.22 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Damage sponge that punishes attackers. Absorb, burst, debuff, repeat.
- **Strength:** Turns enemy aggression into team advantage. Best counter to high-DPS bosses.
- **Weakness:** Passive play pattern. Requires enemies to attack first.

### 11. Mold Mage (Bloomcaster Pilot + Decayer Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Blight Bloom -- homing projectiles that stack escalating debuffs
- **HP:** 4 + 6 + 2 = 12
- **Damage:** Decayer base x 1.25 (low-medium, scaling with stacks)
- **Move Speed:** 6.0 x 0.9 = 5.4 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Ramp-up caster. Stack debuffs at range, damage escalates the longer the fight goes.
- **Strength:** Best scaling damage. Trivializes long boss fights with enough stacks.
- **Weakness:** Slow start. Fast encounters end before stacks matter.

### 12. Rot Oracle (Decayer Pilot + Bloomcaster Gunner)

- **Fusion Ability:** Spore Prophecy -- mark enemies, marked take 3x Bloomcaster damage
- **HP:** 6 + 4 + 2 = 12
- **Damage:** Bloomcaster base x 1.25 (high, x3 on marked)
- **Move Speed:** 5.8 x 0.9 = 5.22 units/s
- **Playstyle:** Execute specialist. Mark priority targets, delete them with amplified projectiles.
- **Strength:** Highest single-target damage in the game on marked targets. Boss killer.
- **Weakness:** Mark has limited uptime. Without marks, damage is merely average.

## Fusion Tier List (General PvE)

| Tier | Fusions | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| S | Rot Oracle, Siege Bloom | Highest damage output, dominant in boss encounters |
| A | Myco-Juggernaut, Plague Fortress, Mold Mage | Strong in all content, reliable |
| B | Trench Spore, Pollen Phantom, Bastion Caster, Wilt Wall | Situationally excellent, map-dependent |
| C | Rot Runner, Blight Blur, Spore Sniper | Require specific conditions or team support to shine |
