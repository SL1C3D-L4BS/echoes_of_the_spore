## Genre Positioning

| Genre Component | Weight in Echoes | Reference Title | What We Take | What We Don't |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Metroidvania | 35% | Hollow Knight / Silksong | Interconnected zones, ability-gated exploration, handcrafted boss design | Backtracking-heavy design, single large map |
| Roguelite | 35% | Hades II / Dead Cells | Per-run boon builds, procedural rooms, meta-progression, "one more run" | Permadeath frustration, solo-only loops |
| Social Co-op | 20% | Lethal Company / Content Warning | Emergent comedy, voice communication as gameplay, viral clip potential | Horror-only tone, no combat depth |
| Persistent World | 10% | Destiny 2 / Terraria | Shared progression visible to all, evolving hub | MMO scale, always-online requirement for solo |

## Uncontested Features

These features have zero direct competition in any shipped or announced title:

| Feature | Nearest Comparison | Why Ours Is Different |
|---|---|---|
| Fungal Fusion (body merge) | It Takes Two (forced co-op) | Physical merger into one entity with split inputs, not just paired puzzles |
| Infection-as-comedy (VOIP DSP) | Phasmophobia (ghost proximity) | Progressive voice degradation tied to power gain, not just proximity fear |
| Community Skill Tree | Destiny 2 seasonal quests | Collective contribution from all server players permanently evolves the world |
| Ecosystem Simulation | Rain World food chain | Enemies hunt each other; room state changes based on time since last visit |
| True Ending Vote | None | Server-wide permanent vote that physically transforms the shared hub |

## Competitive Moat

1. **Technical Moat:** Deterministic rollback netcode with Fusion state machine is 12+ months of engineering. Competitors cannot bolt this on.
2. **Content Moat:** 8 civilizations x 5 boon slots x 4 rarities + 28 Duo Echoes = combinatorial build depth that scales with content drops.
3. **Network Effect Moat:** Community Tree makes the game better the more people play on a server. New players inherit a richer world.
4. **Viral Moat:** Infection voice distortion creates inherently clippable moments that market the game organically.

## Position Risks

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| "Too many genres" confusion | Medium | Lead marketing with Fusion (unique hook), not genre labels |
| Perceived as "just another roguelite" | Medium | Trailer opens with Fusion merge, not solo combat |
| Price resistance at $29.99 | Low | Comparable to Hades II, Mewgenics; proven price point for premium roguelites |
| Platform fragmentation | Low | PC-first launch, console 3 months later |
