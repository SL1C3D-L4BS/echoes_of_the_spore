# ELEVATOR PITCH

## 10-Second Pitch

Four players merge into one body inside a sentient fungal labyrinth. Every run grows a world shared by thousands.

## 30-Second Pitch

**Echoes of the Spore** is a 1-4 player co-op Metroidvania-Roguelite where players physically fuse into a single entity with split controls -- one drives, one shoots. Descend into procedurally generated fungal zones, fight through an ecosystem of creatures that hunt each other, and bring back Echoes that permanently grow a shared hub world visible to every player on your server. As infection spreads through your body, you gain power but lose the ability to communicate with your teammates via real-time voice distortion. The question every run: do you purify and stay human, or embrace the fungus?

## 60-Second Pitch (Publisher/Investor Meeting)

The roguelite market is projected to hit $3.2B by 2033. Hollow Knight: Silksong sold 7M+ units as a singleplayer Metroidvania. Hades II proved roguelites can reach mass-market. But no shipped game combines Metroidvania exploration with co-op roguelite runs and persistent world evolution.

**Echoes of the Spore** sits in that gap. Four players can physically merge into one controllable entity -- unprecedented in shipped games. An infection system simultaneously buffs your combat power and degrades your voice chat through real-time DSP processing, creating inherently clippable comedy moments. Every run deposits resources into a server-wide Community Skill Tree that visually grows the shared hub, unlocks new zones, and spawns NPCs.

We are targeting a $29.99 premium launch with ethical monetization (cosmetic Battle Pass + optional Spore Pass subscription). Conservative projections: 3M units Year 1, $150M gross, 10.5x ROI on a $10M budget.

The team is 8 rising to 25 over a 24-month production cycle using Unity 6.3 LTS with a custom deterministic ECS for rollback netcode.

## Key Talking Points (Ordered by Impact)

1. **Fungal Fusion** -- No shipped game lets 2-4 players merge into one controllable entity with split inputs
2. **Infection Comedy** -- Voice chat degrades in real-time as you gain power, creating viral clip moments
3. **Persistent Server World** -- Your runs grow a world visible to thousands; the Canopy evolves from clearing to city
4. **Proven Market Gap** -- Multiplayer Metroidvania-Roguelite niche is effectively empty
5. **Premium + Ethical** -- $29.99 base, cosmetics only, no loot boxes, no pay-to-win

## Pitch Don'ts

| Never Say | Why | Say Instead |
|---|---|---|
| "Live service" | Implies treadmill/FOMO | "Premium game with seasonal content" |
| "Like Fortnite" | Wrong audience, wrong model | "Like Hades meets Lethal Company" |
| "Revolutionary AI" | Oversells; the AI Director is scoped to Zone 8 | "Dynamic difficulty in the endgame zone" |
| "Next Hollow Knight" | Sets impossible expectations | "In the lineage of Hollow Knight and Dead Cells" |
| "Casual-friendly" | Core audience is mastery-driven | "Accessible depth" |
