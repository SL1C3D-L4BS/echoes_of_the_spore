# UNIQUE SELLING POINTS (USPs)

## USP Hierarchy

Ordered by marketing priority (what leads in trailers, store page, press releases).

### USP 1: Fungal Fusion (Primary Hook)

**The Claim:** 2-4 players physically merge into a single controllable entity with split inputs.

**Why It Matters:**
- Zero shipped games do this. It is unprecedented.
- Creates immediate "I need to try that" reaction in trailers
- Generates emergent comedy (miscommunication between Pilot and Gunner)
- Produces unique content for streamers/YouTubers

**Proof Points:**
- Pilot controls movement, Gunner controls aim/attack, Support handles defensive abilities, Relay manages resources
- 12 unique Fused Class combinations with distinct abilities
- Spore Plan: time-slowed cooperative planning phase during Fusion

**Marketing Execution:**
- Trailer opens with Fusion merge sequence
- Store page hero image: the Fused entity
- Playable demo at conventions always starts with Fusion tutorial

### USP 2: Infection-as-Comedy (Viral Hook)

**The Claim:** As you gain power, your voice chat degrades through real-time DSP processing.

**Why It Matters:**
- Inherently clippable -- streamers will naturally create content
- Creates a genuine gameplay dilemma (power vs. communication)
- Nothing in any shipped game does progressive VOIP distortion tied to gameplay stats

**Proof Points:**
- 6 infection tiers with distinct audio processing
- Tier 51-75: random words replaced with fungal terms
- Tier 76-90: voice replaced with musical tones
- Tier 91-100: pure spore ambience (cannot communicate verbally)

**Marketing Execution:**
- Second trailer beat: two players trying to coordinate while one sounds like a mushroom
- "Infection Replay" feature: post-run, replay your raw audio at any infection level
- Built-in Clip Mode saves 30s of raw + processed audio on command

### USP 3: Persistent Communal World (Retention Hook)

**The Claim:** Every run deposits Echoes into a server-wide tree that permanently evolves the shared hub.

**Why It Matters:**
- Your individual contribution matters to thousands of other players
- The Canopy hub visually grows from a clearing to a fungal city
- Creates FOMO-free investment: the world gets better whether you play or not

**Proof Points:**
- Community Skill Tree: 5 branches x 20 depths = 100 unlock nodes
- Visual evolution: 6 distinct Canopy stages
- True Ending Vote: server-wide permanent decision at max progression
- Cross-player impact: when node 12 unlocks, every player sees the change

**Marketing Execution:**
- Time-lapse trailer of a Canopy evolving over 6 months
- "Your server, your story" messaging
- Post-launch social media: weekly screenshots of different servers at different evolution stages

### USP 4: Build Depth (Mastery Hook)

**The Claim:** 8 civilizations, 5 boon slots, 4 rarities, 28 Duo Echoes, 12 Fused Classes, 15 Heat Conditions.

**Why It Matters:**
- Combinatorial explosion of viable builds
- "I found a combo nobody has tried" fantasy
- Sustains content creators for months/years of theory-crafting

**Proof Points:**
- 4 classes x 3 crests = 12 base loadouts
- 32 Echo Functions, each with 3 modes (Active/Upgrade/Passive)
- Heat system with 50 levels of difficulty scaling

### USP 5: Ecosystem Simulation (Discovery Hook)

**The Claim:** Enemies hunt each other. Rooms change based on time since last visit.

**Why It Matters:**
- Makes the world feel alive and unpredictable
- Rewards exploration and curiosity
- Rain World's food chain was critically acclaimed but never replicated in a roguelite

**Proof Points:**
- Tier 1 enemies feed on environment; Tier 2 hunts Tier 1; Tier 3 hunts Tier 2
- Unvisited rooms simulate forward: Tier 2 may have consumed all Tier 1 (safer but less loot)
- Mycelium Hunters in Zone 7: near-invincible pursuers that investigate sound
