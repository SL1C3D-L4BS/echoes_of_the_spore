# REVENUE MODEL

## Revenue Stream Architecture

### Stream 1: Base Game ($29.99)

| Scenario | Units (Y1) | Gross Revenue | Platform Fee (30%) | Net |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 3,000,000 | $89,970,000 | $26,991,000 | $62,979,000 |
| Target | 4,000,000 | $119,960,000 | $35,988,000 | $83,972,000 |
| Stretch | 5,000,000 | $149,950,000 | $44,985,000 | $104,965,000 |

**Pricing justification:**
- Hades II: $29.99 (multi-million sales)
- Mewgenics: $29.99 (1M week 1)
- Dead Cells: $24.99 (15M lifetime, launched at lower price in 2018)
- Slay the Spire 2: $24.99 (3M week 1)

$29.99 is the established premium indie roguelite price point. We are not a $39.99 game (that pushes into AA territory). We are not a $19.99 game (leaves money on the table given feature depth).

### Stream 2: Battle Pass ($9.99/season, 4 seasons/year)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Price | $9.99 per season |
| Duration | 3 months (aligned with seasonal content) |
| Attach rate (Y1) | 30% of active players |
| Content | 100 tiers: cosmetic skins, emotes, titles, spray tags, loading screens |
| Free track | 30 tiers (ensures all players feel progress) |
| Premium track | 70 additional tiers |
| No gameplay items | Zero functions, boons, crests, or stats in Battle Pass |
| Catch-up | Purchased pass unlocks retroactively from tier 1 |

| Scenario | Active Players | Attach Rate | Seasons | Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 900K avg | 30% | 4 | $10,789,200 |
| Target | 1,200K avg | 35% | 4 | $16,783,200 |
| Stretch | 1,500K avg | 40% | 4 | $23,976,000 |

### Stream 3: Spore Pass ($4.99/month subscription)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Price | $4.99/month |
| Benefits | 2x Echo contribution multiplier, exclusive cosmetic mutations, branch voting rights, monthly stats report |
| Does NOT gate | Zone access, boons, functions, matchmaking, Heat, Codex |
| Conversion rate | 15% of MAU |
| Churn rate | 10% monthly |
| Avg subscription length | 6 months |

| Scenario | MAU | Conv. Rate | Avg Months | Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 600K | 15% | 6 | $2,696,400 |
| Target | 800K | 18% | 7 | $5,029,920 |
| Stretch | 1,000K | 20% | 8 | $7,984,000 |

### Stream 4: Cosmetic Store (Direct Purchase)

| Item Type | Price Range | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Character skins | $4.99-$9.99 | Alternate class appearances, infection variants |
| Weapon skins | $2.99-$4.99 | Blade effects, projectile trails |
| Emotes | $1.99-$2.99 | Victory poses, idle animations |
| Hub decorations | $1.99-$3.99 | Personal housing items (visible to server) |
| Bundle packs | $14.99-$24.99 | Themed collections (e.g., "Pyreth Collection") |

**Anti-predatory rules:**
- All items visible before purchase (no loot boxes, no gacha)
- No limited-time exclusives that never return
- Seasonal items archived and available for direct purchase next year
- In-client lifetime spend tracker visible in settings

| Scenario | Spender % | Avg Spend/Spender/Year | Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 20% | $12 | $7,200,000 |
| Target | 25% | $15 | $15,000,000 |
| Stretch | 30% | $18 | $27,000,000 |

### Stream 5: Brand Collaborations

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
