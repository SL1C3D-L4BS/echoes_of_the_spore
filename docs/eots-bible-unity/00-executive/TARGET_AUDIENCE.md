# TARGET AUDIENCE

## Primary Persona: "The Spelunker" (60% of projected revenue)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 18-34 |
| Gender | 65% male, 30% female, 5% non-binary |
| Geography | NA (40%), EU (30%), Asia-Pacific (20%), LATAM (10%) |
| Platform | PC primary (70%), PS5 (15%), Xbox (10%), Switch 2 (5%) |
| Income | $25K-$75K annual (or equivalent spending power) |
| Play time | 10-20 hours/week |
| Discovery | YouTube recommendations, Twitch, Reddit, friend recommendations |
| Spend pattern | Buys $29.99 base game, 50% chance of Battle Pass purchase |
| Favorite games | Hades II, Dead Cells, Silksong, Celeste, Blasphemous |
| Core motivation | Mechanical mastery, optimization, social proof of skill |
| Content behavior | Clips impressive runs on TikTok/YouTube Shorts, shares build screenshots |

**What The Spelunker Wants:**
1. Tight, responsive combat that rewards skill investment
2. Deep build variety that enables personal expression
3. Clear progression systems that show improvement over time
4. Challenge scaling (Heat system) to prove mastery
5. Social proof: visible achievements, leaderboards, prestige cosmetics

**What Drives The Spelunker Away:**
- Input lag > 50ms
- RNG that feels unfair (no player agency in boon selection)
- Mandatory grinding for gameplay power
- Pay-to-win monetization
- Lack of endgame depth

### Spelunker Sub-Segments

| Sub-Segment | % of Spelunkers | Key Difference | Design Implication |
|---|---|---|---|
| Solo Spelunker | 35% | Prefers solo runs, avoids co-op | Solo must be viable and rewarding; Micro-Plan for solo Spore Plan |
| Duo Spelunker | 40% | Plays with one consistent partner | 2P Fusion is the "default" Fusion experience; balance around duo |
| Squad Spelunker | 25% | Always plays in 3-4P groups | 4P Fusion roles must all feel meaningful |

## Secondary Persona: "The Architect" (25% of projected revenue)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 25-40 |
| Gender | 55% male, 40% female, 5% non-binary |
| Platform | PC (85%), console (15%) |
| Play time | 15-30 hours/week (high engagement) |
| Spend pattern | Base game + Spore Pass subscriber (highest ARPU segment) |
| Favorite games | Factorio, Dwarf Fortress, Terraria, Stardew Valley, Path of Exile |
| Core motivation | Systemic impact, long-term contribution, visible legacy |
| Content behavior | Writes guides, manages community spreadsheets, posts to wikis |

**What The Architect Wants:**
1. Community Tree progression as primary endgame
2. Visible impact of their contributions on the server
3. Branch voting as strategic decision-making
4. Data: statistics, contribution history, server comparisons
5. Social organization: leading branch priority campaigns

**What Drives The Architect Away:**
- Their contributions feeling invisible
- Community Tree pacing too slow (no visible progress per session)
- Community Tree pacing too fast (finishes before they invest)
- Lack of data/analytics about their impact

## Tertiary Persona: "The Socialite" (15% of projected revenue)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 16-28 |
| Gender | Even split |
| Platform | PC (60%), console (30%), cloud-mobile spectate (10%) |
| Play time | 5-10 hours/week (lower engagement, higher social intensity) |
| Spend pattern | Base game; low Battle Pass attach; purchases meme cosmetics |
| Favorite games | Lethal Company, Content Warning, PEAK, Among Us, Fall Guys |
| Core motivation | Social moments, laughter, shared experiences, content creation |
| Content behavior | Streams on Twitch, posts clips daily, plays "for the content" |

**What The Socialite Wants:**
1. Fusion chaos with friends
2. Infection voice distortion producing comedy
3. Easy clip/share tools
4. Low barrier to join friends (quick matchmaking)
5. Cosmetics that are funny/absurd, not just cool

**What Drives The Socialite Away:**
- Runs that are too long (> 30 min before comedy payoff)
- Steep difficulty that gates the comedy (can't reach high infection)
- No way to share moments in-game
- Friends can't join easily
