# 21 — MONETIZATION, BUSINESS MODEL & ETHICAL CONSTRAINTS

## Revenue Streams
| Stream | Price | Projected % of Revenue | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Base Game | $29.99 | 45% | Premium purchase, all gameplay content |
| Battle Pass | $9.99/season (4/year) | 25% | Cosmetics, emotes, titles. No gameplay advantage. |
| Spore Pass | $4.99/month | 15% | 2x Echo contribution, cosmetic mutations, branch voting, monthly stats report |
| Cosmetic Store | $1.99-$14.99/item | 10% | Direct purchase, no loot boxes, no randomization |
| Brand Collabs | Partnership deals | 5% | Nike "Sneaker Spores," Monster Energy "Toxin Bloom," etc. Cosmetic only. |

## Anti-Predatory Design Principles
1. **No gameplay advantage from purchases.** Battle Pass and Store contain cosmetics ONLY.
2. **No loot boxes.** All purchases are direct. See exactly what you buy.
3. **No limited-time FOMO on purchases.** Seasonal cosmetics return in annual archive shop.
4. **Transparent spend tracking.** In-client lifetime expenditure display.
5. **Spore Pass accelerates contribution, NOT access.** Non-pass holders get all tree unlocks.
6. **No pay-to-skip.** Cannot buy Echo Functions, Crests, Boons, or Heat clears.
7. **Parental controls.** Spending limits configurable in account settings.
