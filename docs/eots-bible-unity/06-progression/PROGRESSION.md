# 06 — PROGRESSION, PERSISTENCE & META-PROGRESSION

## Per-Run Progression (Ephemeral)
- Echo Boons: acquired during delve, lost on death/completion
- Echo Functions: persistent (unlocked permanently, equipped per-run)
- Infection Level: accumulates during delve, can be purified between runs (costs Echoes)
- Silk Gauge: fills during combat, resets per room for heal/ability usage

## Per-Account Progression (Permanent)
- Echo Functions: unlocked by finding them in delves. 32 total across 4 classes.
- Crests: unlocked by defeating specific bosses or completing Codex entries. 12 total.
- Pact of the Mycelium: unlocked after first full clear. 15 Conditions, max Heat 50.
- Echo Codex: 300+ entries, 5 categories. Progress is permanent.
- Cosmetic unlocks: from Heat milestones, Codex completion, Battle Pass, store.

## Per-Server Progression (Communal)
- Community Skill Tree: 5 branches × 20 depths = 100 nodes.
- Cost formula: `cost(d) = floor(1000 × 1.8^d)` where d = depth (1-20).
- Estimated timeline: Depth 1-5 in week 1, Depth 10 by month 1, Depth 20 by month 6+.
- Echo contribution is individual but effect is global.
- Spore Pass ($4.99/mo) grants 2x contribution multiplier. Does NOT gate content.
- Branch voting: Spore Pass holders vote on priority branch each week.

## Heat System (Pact of the Mycelium)

15 toggleable Conditions, each with 1-5 ranks. Each rank = 1 Heat. Max Heat = 50.
Heat multiplies Echo drops: `echo_mult = 1.0 + (heat × 0.05)`. At Heat 50: 3.5x Echoes.
