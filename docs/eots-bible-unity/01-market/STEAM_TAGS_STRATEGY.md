# Steam Tags Strategy

## Primary Tags (Top 5 — drives discovery)

1. **Roguelite** — 14,200+ titles, high search volume, our primary genre
2. **Metroidvania** — 3,800+ titles, dedicated audience, underserved in co-op
3. **Co-op** — 11,000+ titles, essential for multiplayer discovery
4. **Action** — broadest reach tag
5. **2D** — filters out 3D competitors

## Secondary Tags (6-10)

6. **Online Co-Op** — distinguishes from local-only
7. **Souls-like** — committed combat, punishing but fair
8. **Procedural Generation** — roguelite expectation
9. **Dark Fantasy** — atmospheric positioning
10. **Indie** — community preference tag

## Tertiary Tags (11-15)

11. **PvP** — Rite of Echoes discovery
12. **Horror** — Zone 7 Mycelium Hunters, atmosphere
13. **Multiplayer** — broad catch-all
14. **Atmospheric** — bioluminescent void aesthetic
15. **Difficult** — Heat system, skill ceiling

## Tag Combination Discovery Paths

| Player searches for... | Tags matching | Competitors in that intersection |
|------------------------|---------------|----------------------------------|
| "roguelite co-op" | Roguelite + Co-op | Risk of Rain 2, Gunfire Reborn | 
| "metroidvania co-op" | Metroidvania + Co-op | **< 10 titles** (blue ocean) |
| "roguelite metroidvania" | Roguelite + Metroidvania | Dead Cells (partial), ~20 titles |
| "co-op horror roguelite" | Co-op + Horror + Roguelite | Lethal Company (partial) |
| "metroidvania co-op 2D" | Metroidvania + Co-op + 2D | **< 5 titles** |

## Key Insight
The intersection of **Metroidvania + Co-op** has fewer than 10 Steam titles. Adding **Roguelite** to that reduces it to effectively zero shipped competitors. We own this tag intersection.
