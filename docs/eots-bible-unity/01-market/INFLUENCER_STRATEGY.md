# Influencer Strategy

## Tier System

### Tier 1: Anchor Partners (5-10 creators, 1M+ subscribers)
- **Relationship:** Early access builds, exclusive reveals, co-development input
- **Compensation:** Revenue share on referral codes (5%), early access 30 days pre-launch
- **Target Creators:** Markiplier (horror/co-op), Dunkey (indie advocacy), NorthernLion (roguelite authority), Lirik (variety/co-op), Jerma (comedy)
- **Content Type:** "First look" videos, co-op sessions, Fusion highlight reels

### Tier 2: Community Builders (20-30 creators, 100K-1M subscribers)
- **Relationship:** Beta access, feedback channel, build guide creators
- **Compensation:** Free game + cosmetic bundle, affiliate codes
- **Target Niches:** Roguelite specialists (Olexa, Retromation), Metroidvania channels (Relyea, Skippi), Co-op channels (Stumpt, Let's Game It Out)
- **Content Type:** Build guides, tier lists, lore deep dives, Heat challenge runs

### Tier 3: Grassroots (100+ creators, 10K-100K subscribers)
- **Relationship:** Review codes, Discord community
- **Compensation:** Free game
- **Target:** Small roguelite/indie creators, TikTok clip creators, speedrunners
- **Content Type:** Short clips, TikTok moments, speedrun attempts

## Viral Mechanic: Infection Voice Clips

The Spore-Talk DSP system is designed to be inherently clippable:
- **Clip Mode** saves last 30 seconds of raw + processed audio
- **Infection Replay** lets players re-process any clip at any infection level
- **Built-in sharing** exports directly to clipboard/file
- **Watermark:** Small "Echoes of the Spore" logo + Steam link in corner

### Expected Viral Loop
1. Player's voice degrades hilariously during intense combat
2. Partner laughs, triggers Clip Mode
3. Clip exports with game branding
4. Posted to TikTok/YouTube Shorts/Twitter
5. Viewers see the game name, wishlist on Steam
6. New players create their own clips → loop repeats
