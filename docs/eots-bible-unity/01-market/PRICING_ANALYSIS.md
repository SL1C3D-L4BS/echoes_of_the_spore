$29.99 positions us:
- Above Hollow Knight ($14.99) — justified by multiplayer, replayability, content volume
- Equal to Hades II ($29.99) — appropriate for scope and polish target
- Below AAA ($59.99-$69.99) — indie positioning, impulse-buy range

## Monetization Tiers

### Tier 0: Base Game ($29.99) — No Further Spend Required
- Full 8 zones, 4 classes, 12 Crests, all Echo Functions
- Online co-op, Fusion, Spore-Talk
- Community Tree participation
- All gameplay content, no paywalls

### Tier 1: Battle Pass ($9.99/season, 4/year)
- 100 levels of cosmetic-only rewards
- Free track: 30 items (basic recolors, titles)
- Premium track: 70 items (animated skins, Fusion VFX, Infection overlays)
- Cannot buy levels — must play to progress
- Season duration: 90 days

### Tier 2: Spore Pass ($4.99/month)
- 2x Community Tree contribution multiplier
- Weekly branch priority vote
- Exclusive cosmetic border
- Does NOT gate any gameplay content
- Cancellable anytime

### Tier 3: Cosmetic Store (Direct Purchase)
- Individual skins: $2.99-$7.99
- Fusion skin sets: $9.99
- Infection VFX packs: $4.99
- Narrator voice packs: $3.99 (alternate Mycelium Voice actors)
- NO loot boxes, NO randomized purchases

## Revenue Model Principles
1. **No pay-to-win** — all purchasable items are cosmetic
2. **No pay-to-skip** — cannot buy Echo Functions, Crests, Heat levels, or Codex entries
3. **No loot boxes** — all purchases are known items at known prices
4. **No FOMO** — Battle Pass items return to store 1 season later at premium price
5. **Respect the player** — premium price buys the full game, everything else is optional
