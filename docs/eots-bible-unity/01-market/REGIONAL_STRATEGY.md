## Localization Requirements

### Full Localization (UI + Subtitles + Store Page)
EN, FR, DE, ES, IT, JA, KO, PT-BR, ZH-CN, ZH-TW

### Partial Localization (UI + Store Page Only)
PL, RU, TH, ID, TR, AR

### Voice (Mycelium Voice Narrator)
EN only at launch. JA and KO voice packs as Tier 2 DLC ($3.99 each).

### Spore-Talk Voice Corruption
Language-agnostic by design — DSP effects (pitch shift, resonance, word replacement) work on any language input. Word replacement dictionary needs per-language fungal term lists (50 words per language).

## Cultural Considerations

| Region | Concern | Mitigation |
|--------|---------|------------|
| China | Horror content, skeleton imagery | Alternative enemy skins, no visible bones |
| Germany | USK rating, violence level | Ensure USK 16 (not 18) — no excessive gore |
| Japan | Body horror (Fusion) sensitivity | Frame as "cooperation" not "body horror" in marketing |
| MENA | Infection/disease themes | Sensitive post-COVID — frame as fantasy fungi, not disease |
| South Korea | Loot box regulations | No loot boxes in design — already compliant |
