# Post-Launch Content Roadmap

## Year 1: Seasons of the Spore

### Season 1 — "Awakening" (Launch + 90 days)
- **Battle Pass:** 100 tiers, infection-themed cosmetics
- **New Content:** 3 new Echo Boons per class (12 total), 2 new Duo Echoes
- **Event:** First server-wide Rite of Echoes tournament
- **QoL:** Replay system, improved matchmaking, accessibility updates
- **Balance:** First major balance patch based on analytics data

### Season 2 — "Convergence" (Month 4-6)
- **Battle Pass:** 100 tiers, Fusion-themed cosmetics
- **New Content:** 5th class teaser (community vote on archetype), 2 new Crests for existing classes
- **Event:** Cross-server Rite of Echoes (top 4 teams from each server)
- **Feature:** Spectator mode for Rite of Echoes
- **Map:** 2 new room templates per zone

### Season 3 — "Corruption" (Month 7-9)
- **Battle Pass:** 100 tiers, civilization-themed cosmetics (one per civ)
- **New Content:** 5th class launch (full class + 3 Crests + 8 Echo Functions + 4 new Fused combos)
- **Event:** Halloween event — Zone 7 Hunters escape to all zones for 1 week
- **Feature:** Custom lobby creation with Heat presets
- **Balance:** Heat system rebalance, new Heat Conditions (5 more)

### Season 4 — "Ascension" (Month 10-12)
- **Battle Pass:** 100 tiers, prestige cosmetics
- **New Content:** Zone 9 tease (Expansion branch Depth 20 requirement), 4 new Legendary Echoes
- **Event:** Year-end community celebration, server milestone rewards
- **Feature:** Leaderboards (Heat speedruns, Codex completion, Rite rankings)
- **Anniversary:** All Season 1-3 Battle Pass items available in store

## Year 2: The Deep

### Major Content Update — "The Undergrowth" (Month 13-15)
- **Zone 9:** Full new zone unlocked via Community Tree
- **Hyper-Fusion:** 5-player fusion (Expansion Depth 20 unlock)
- **New Civilization:** 9th civilization with boons, Duo Echoes, Legendary
- **New Boss:** Zone 9 boss
- **Price:** Free update (included in base game)

### Year 2 Seasons (4 more seasons following same template)
- 2 new classes by end of Year 2 (6 total)
- 8 new Crests
- 48 new Echo Functions
- Monthly community events
- Ongoing balance and QoL
