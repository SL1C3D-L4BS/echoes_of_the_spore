# 09 — NARRATIVE, LORE & ENVIRONMENTAL STORYTELLING

## Story Structure

Echoes tells its story through three channels, in order of priority:

1. **Environmental storytelling (primary):** Fossil Murals, Canopy evolution, zone atmosphere
2. **Reactive narration (secondary):** The Mycelium Voice (800-1200 lines)
3. **Codex entries (tertiary):** Unlocked by using Functions in all 3 modes + discovering murals

There are NO cutscenes in the traditional sense. The only "cutscene" is the True Ending vote sequence.

## The True Ending

Lore branch Depth 20 triggers a 48-hour server-wide vote: FEED (spread the Mycelium) or STARVE (let it die). Simple majority, permanent result. Both outcomes unlock different post-game content and visually transform the Canopy.
