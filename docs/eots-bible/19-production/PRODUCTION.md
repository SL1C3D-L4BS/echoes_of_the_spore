# 19 — PRODUCTION ROADMAP, STAFFING & MILESTONE PLAN

## Phase Schedule

| Phase | Months | Duration | Team Size | Key Deliverables |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-Production | 1-4 | 4 mo | 8 | Core tech prototype, art style lock, design doc final, vertical slice scope |
| Vertical Slice | 5-10 | 6 mo | 15 | 2 zones playable, 2 classes, Fusion working, Spore-Talk prototype, local+online co-op |
| Alpha | 11-16 | 6 mo | 20 | All 8 zones, all 4 classes, 12 Fused Classes, Community Tree, Boon system, Gen-AI pipeline |
| Beta | 17-20 | 4 mo | 25 | Closed beta (10K), stress test, balance pass, console porting, certification prep |
| Polish | 21-22 | 2 mo | 25 | Open beta, performance optimization, accessibility pass, localization |
| Launch | 23-24 | 2 mo | 25+ QA | Gold master, Season 1 Battle Pass, first brand collab, marketing push |
| Live Service | 25+ | Ongoing | 15 | Seasonal content, balance patches, new zones/classes/Fused Classes |
